About me

My name is Stevie Gill (aka caffeinated pixels) and I'm a Brit living in Toronto, Canada.

I'm currently teaching myself web development through a combination of freeCodeCamp, Codecademy and Scrimba. You can checkout some of my completed web projects below, all of which I developed as part of the excellent freeCodeCamp curriculum.

Ohhhhh... what else is there to say? Well, I’m a keen amateur photographer and photoblogger. I like to write about indie and retro video games and I'm an Amiga emulation enthusiast (amongst other 8- and 16-bit systems). And I love books and documentaries about video game history and culture.

I'm also huge fan of pixel art and I'm hopelessly addicted to synthwave and chiptune music. I live for coffee and cake and I once dyed my hair red. I occasionally play guitar... very badly!
